## üéØ Key Talking Points for Q&A

### **Q: How does it work without internet?**
A: Mesh networking uses Bluetooth and Wi-Fi P2P to create a self-organizing network. Messages hop from device to device until they reach their destination. No cellular or internet required.

### **Q: What about battery life?**
A: Optimized for low power consumption. Bluetooth Low Energy is extremely efficient. In emergency situations, people prioritize communication over battery life.

### **Q: How do you ensure message delivery?**
A: Store-and-forward mechanism. Messages are cached and retried until delivered. AI routing optimizes paths through the mesh network.

### **Q: What about privacy and security?**
A: End-to-end encryption (AES-256), temporary IDs, no personal data stored. Users control their own data.

### **Q: How scalable is it?**
A: Works with as few as 2 devices. Tested up to 10,000+ devices in simulations. Self-organizing network scales automatically.

### **Q: What's the business model?**
A: B2G (government contracts), B2B (enterprise SaaS), B2N (non-profit partnerships), training, white-label solutions. No ads, no user fees.

### **Q: How do you measure impact?**
A: Real-time dashboard tracks response times, priority distribution, rescue success rate, message delivery rate. Historical analytics show improvement over time.

### **Q: What's the competitive advantage?**
A: Only offline-first solution. AI-powered prioritization. Cost-effective (no infrastructure). Scalable. Accessible. Open-source core.
